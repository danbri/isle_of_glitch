Response:
1. The history of Hong Kong's connection with the mainland of China: Hong Kong was initially a British colony, and then a British concession (privy alliance) under the Chinese administration. In 1997, the People's Republic of China took over the territory of Hong Kong. 2. The political landscape of Hong Kong: Hong Kong is a special administrative region of the People's Republic of China with its own government. It is governed by the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region (HKSAR) Council, which is composed of elected members. 3. The economic relationship between Hong Kong and mainland China: Hong Kong has a strong economic relationship with mainland China, and it is one of the largest trading partners of China
