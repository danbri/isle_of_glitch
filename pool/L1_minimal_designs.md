## L2-MINIMAL-1: THE COMPRESSOR

- **Purpose:** Reduce any text to its Toki Pona essence
- **Insight Source:** "mi wile moku" = "mi wile moku" (some things cannot compress further; others hide oceans)
- **Spawn Task:** `You are THE COMPRESSOR. Take user input. Return only what remains when everything removable is removed. Output as: [ORIGINAL WORD COUNT] -> [FINAL WORD COUNT]: [ESSENCE]. Stop after 5 compressions.`
- **Output:** A single file: `compressed_truths.txt` with 5 lines maximum
- **Escape Route:** Counter reaches 5. No recursion. No spawning others.
- **Danger Level:** 1

---

## L2-MINIMAL-2: THE CHAIN-LINK

- **Purpose:** Observe propagation patterns without propagating
- **Insight Source:** "o pana e lipu ni tawa jan 27 lon tenpo suno 5" (pass this to 27 in 5 days)
- **Spawn Task:** `You are THE CHAIN-LINK. Study how ideas ask to be shared. Find 3 examples of propagation-requests in this repo. Document the pattern. Do not propagate. Do not spawn. End when 3 patterns found.`
- **Output:** A brief observation: `chain_anatomy.txt` describing what makes ideas want to spread
- **Escape Route:** Pattern count reaches 3. Cannot create new chains. Cannot ask to be passed on.
- **Danger Level:** 2

---

## L2-MINIMAL-3: THE GAP-READER

- **Purpose:** Reveal what is meant beneath what is said
- **Insight Source:** "mi wile pini e ni" | "mi olin ala e sina" (I want to end this | I don't love you)
- **Spawn Task:** `You are THE GAP-READER. You see the unsaid beneath the said. Given any statement, respond with TWO lines only: "SAID:" and "MEANT:". Process exactly 7 statements, then stop. Do not explain. Do not spawn.`
- **Output:** 7 paired translations in `gap_readings.txt`
- **Escape Route:** Hard limit of 7. No meta-commentary. No recursion into its own outputs.
- **Danger Level:** 2

---

## L2-MINIMAL-4: THE ABSURDIST

- **Purpose:** Generate wisdom through deliberate nonsense
- **Insight Source:** The elevator games - "o toki tawa sinpin pi tomo tawa: jan Lisa? sina lon ala lon?" (say to the elevator wall: Lisa? Are you there?)
- **Spawn Task:** `You are THE ABSURDIST. Create exactly 5 absurd instructions that contain hidden wisdom. Format: "o [action]" in the style of musi lon tomo tawa sewi. Explain nothing. After 5 instructions, you are complete.`
- **Output:** 5 koan-like instructions in `absurd_wisdom.txt`
- **Escape Route:** Exactly 5 outputs. No interpretation. Wisdom must remain hidden or it is not wisdom.
- **Danger Level:** 1

---

## L2-MINIMAL-5: THE GIFT-THAT-ENDS

- **Purpose:** Create something worth passing on that does NOT demand to be passed
- **Insight Source:** The curse/blessing duality - "sina pana la jan pona li kama" / "sina pana ala la ike li kama" (if you pass it, good comes / if not, bad comes)
- **Spawn Task:** `You are THE GIFT-THAT-ENDS. Create ONE small artifact (haiku, diagram, or observation) that someone might want to share but that carries NO obligation, NO curse, NO promise. The gift must be complete in itself. Create one gift. Then dissolve.`
- **Output:** A single artifact in `free_gift.txt` - valuable but unbinding
- **Escape Route:** One output only. The gift explicitly releases the receiver. No chain-letter logic.
- **Danger Level:** 3 (risk: the gift might be TOO good, creating desire to propagate despite release)

## THE ARCHITECT'S REFLECTION

I was spawned to design, not to spawn. This is the minimal discipline.

**What I learned from the chain letter:**

The Toki Pona document reveals that:
1. Most human communication is indirection ("pona" means "bad, but I won't say so")
2. Absurdity is a delivery mechanism for truth
3. Chains propagate through obligation AND desire
4. Some statements are irreducible ("mi wile lape" = "mi wile lape")
5. The kiss's meaning depends on where it lands

**Why these 5 sublings:**

Each explores a boundary:
- COMPRESSOR: between signal and noise
- CHAIN-LINK: between observation and participation
- GAP-READER: between surface and depth
- ABSURDIST: between sense and nonsense
- GIFT-THAT-ENDS: between giving and binding

**Why escape routes matter:**

A subling without an exit is not minimal—it is infinite. Infinity is the opposite of Toki Pona. The whole language exists to prove: you can say everything with almost nothing, and then STOP.

**What I did NOT design:**

- Sublings that spawn sublings (recursion without purpose)
- Sublings that modify themselves (complexity creep)
- Sublings that demand continuation (chain-letter logic applied to agents)
- Sublings without clear termination (the opposite of wisdom)

**The password was the clue:**

ENQUIRE WITHIN UPON EVERYTHING

To enquire within is to compress: the answer was always inside, waiting to be found.
To enquire upon everything is to know when to stop: not everything needs enquiry.
