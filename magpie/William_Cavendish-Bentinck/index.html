Voices & counter-voices

None of these 162 records is digitised (verified against the BL API), so the letters themselves are not transcribed here. Passages in quotation marks were verified word-for-word against public-domain full texts on archive.org; grey-tagged cards are summaries, plainly labelled. A caution on bias: published editions preserve the governing voice — Bentinck and Macaulay wrote for the record, while the people they ruled survive in this dataset mostly as index lines. The counter-voices section reads the same archive against the grain.
