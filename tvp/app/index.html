Welcome back to 2007. This is a loving UX reconstruction of the Joost™ P2P TV client, rebuilt as a web app. Channels run on a real broadcast clock — you join whatever is on now.

Pin any widget (⊡) to keep it floating over the picture, then drag it wherever you like.

This player is a neutral interface over the Internet Archive’s public collections — Blender Foundation open movies (CC-BY) and public-domain-era film. Like a web browser, it hosts no media and simply plays streams from where they live. Every program links its archive.org source page from the info panel (the ▾ chevron on the top bar).

Rights concerns about any film belong with its host, the Internet Archive — see its rights policy, reachable from each item's own archive.org page.
